01 — THE STUFF

Classic rock, R&B, and a touch of country.

A five-piece built to keep a room full. Standards from the Southern rock canon, a few country classics, some R&B you'd forgotten you loved, and one slow burn to close it out. The set list shifts with the room — that's the whole point of a cover band that's played the same songs a thousand times.

04 — THE BAND

Five guys, two of them named Bill.

Steve "Buck" Dennison

Lead vocals · Guitar · Booking

Frontman, calls the gigs, owns the room. The guy you talk to when you're booking. Want to hear it? He'll probably know it — request just about anything and chances are he's got it ready.

Frank Gatto

Guitar

Been playing since he was a kid. Luthier at Gatto Guitars. Three decades behind the counter at music stores — selling, teaching, playing out. One of the best guitar players you'll ever meet. Most people know him when they see him.

Jim Mihalovich

Drums · Backing vocals

Been playing drums since the '80s. Local bands, road gigs, every kind of room you can name. The kind of player who makes the rest of the band sound tighter without ever asking for the spotlight.

Bill Thorpe

Bass · Vocals

A working musician for over 40 years. Multi-instrumentalist — bass, fiddle, and whatever else the night calls for. Wedding bands, bars, VFW halls, American Legion rooms, festival stages. He's played them all and he still shows up early.

Billy Thorpe

Guitar · Vocals · Recording

Records the gigs, posts the clips, runs the website. Plays the high strings.
