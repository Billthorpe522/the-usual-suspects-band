01 — THE STUFF

Classic rock, R&B, and a touch of country.

A five-piece built to keep a room full. Standards from the Southern rock canon, a few country classics, some R&B you'd forgotten you loved, and one slow burn to close it out. The set list shifts with the room — that's the whole point of a cover band that's played the same songs a thousand times.

05 — THE BAND

Meet the Band

Steve "Buck" Dennison

Lead vocals · Guitar · Booking

Frontman, calls the gigs, owns the room. The guy you talk to when you're booking. Want to hear it? He'll probably know it — request just about anything and chances are he's got it ready.

Frank Gatto

Guitar

Three decades of playing out, teaching, and selling behind the counter at music stores — and a luthier at Gatto Guitars. One of the best guitar players you'll ever meet, and most people in the room already know it.

Jim "Crash" Mihalovich

Drums · Backing vocals

Been playing drums his whole life — since before he could reach the pedals. A veteran, a lifelong baseball fan, and the kind of drummer every band wishes they had. Local bands, road gigs, every kind of room you can name. The reason the rest of the band sounds tight without ever asking for the spotlight.

Bill Thorpe

Bass · Vocals

A working musician for over 40 years. Multi-instrumentalist — bass, violin, and whatever else the night calls for. Wedding bands, bars, VFW halls, American Legion rooms, festival stages. He's played them all and he still shows up early.

Billy Thorpe

Guitar · Recording & Video

An all-around guitarist who handles the band's recording and video — and built this website. Son of bassist Bill Thorpe; every time he's on stage, there's a Thorpe holding down the bass end too.
